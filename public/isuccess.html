Fantastic work and incredible progress from this one! When you combine consistency, sound programming, and determination you can achieve great results! Keep it up, Jasper! This is only the beginning.

Ramza S.

Awesome results from our client Ramza for losing 10kg for her engagement in 10 weeks! The amazing thing, of course, is the speed at which she lost 10kg in a healthy manner.
Ramza's time was very restricted: she was attending university classes, revising for the approaching exam period and coursework deadlines, she had engagement planning and family commitments which required her to fly out of the country twice for, and still further various miscellaneous tasks.
Despite all of this, she made the time to train and eat the right way to reach her fitness goals - the key to her fitness success. She knew her time was limited and she made the right choices and followed the program.
A big congratulations to your beautiful engagement, Ramza. Wishing you and your partner all the happiness in the world!
