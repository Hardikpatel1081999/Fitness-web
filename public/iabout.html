About Us


F2 Fitness was founded in 2002 as a family owned and operated business. The F2 Fitness founders didn’t want it to be just another gym - they wanted to be the best in the industry and set their minds to doing so! Since its birth, F2 Fitness has grown into one of Mumbai's largest fitness center having helped over 50,000 customers live longer, happier and healthier.

F2 fitness is a group fitness studio and a personal training studio where we focus on different aspects of fitness People attending our group classes are exposed to a social, fun and safe environment.
A variety of class formats keep members motivated and interested as well as get different instructor styles and music selection Most of them attending group classes don’t feel lost with the workout as during an exercise they are given name, the range wat muscle group, safety cues on form and technique and also motivated by an instructor and music to make performance better As louder the music harder the beat better your workout. And the most important part is they don’t feel alone.
